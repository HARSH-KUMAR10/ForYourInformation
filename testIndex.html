Diwali : Festival Of Lights
Diwali, Deepavali or Divali is the Indian festival of lights, usually lasting five days and celebrated during the Hindu Lunisolar month Kartika (between mid-October and mid-November).
Diwali symbolizes the spiritual "victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance"

Indian Children's Day
Children's Day is celebrated across India to increase awareness of the rights, care and education of children.
It is celebrated on 14 November every year as a tribute to India's First Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru. Nehru Ji considered children as real strength of a nation and foundation of society
